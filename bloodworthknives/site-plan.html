Overview

Purpose

My idea for this website is to create an easy-to-use website dedicated to my grandfathers knife making business. Customers will be able to buy knives that he has already built from this website. Have you ever dreamed of owning a unique knife that no one else has? Let us know what your dream knife is, and we will get you a quote for how much is will cost to make it. With over 35 years of experience, we are the best in the custom knife making business.

Audience

Our audience is knife collectors and enthusiasts who are looking to add beautiful works of art to their collection. We aim to please knife buyers, young and old. We have a perfect knife for every age. We sell a range of knives that are useful in almost every situation of life, from kitchen knives to hunting knives.

Home page

OUR MISSION: Our purpose for making knives is for you to enjoy your experience with Bloodworth Custom Knives. From the first time you look at it, to when hold your knife, we want you to be confident and have pride in knowing that your Bloodworth is everything you expected it to be and more. We are a company that focuses heavily on always having the right tool for the job. SERVICES THAT WE PROVIDE: SHEATHS: Has one of your sheaths come apart? We happen to enjoy working with leather and will be happy to make a sheath for your knife. We use 8oz cowhide, formed to fit your knife and hand stitched. Each one of our knives comes with a custom-made sheath designed to fit your knife perfectly. KNIFE REPAIR: Do you have a special knife that has been in your family for generations and needs repair? If you have an old knife, knife blade or a great knife that needs to be restored, let us know what your idea is, and we might be able help fix it for you. We enjoy restoring knives to their former glory. KNIFE CLASSES! Have you ever wanted to learn how to make your own knives? We offer a verity of knife classes depending on what you would like to learn. One option we have is a short two-week class where your will learn the basics of how to make a knife. This is the perfect gift for anyone who has always dreamed of owning a knife they made themselves. Our other class is a six-week class that meets twice a week. This class is for the person who wants to learn the KNIFE BUSINESS. You will learn things like KNIFE DESIGN, HOLLOW GRINDING, FLAT GRINDING, MAKING SHEATHS, HEAT TREATNG AND MUCH MORE. You will build your own knife and have six or more knives heat treated and ready to finish.

About Us

When our founder first started to make knives, he didn't start making knives as a hobby or to have something to do. He was living in Alaska and bought some remote property between Anchorage and Fairbanks. He knew that he needed some way to make money or trade for the things he would need. He hunted and fished all his life and knew how important a good knife was. So, he decided to start building custom knives. He started Bloodworth Custom Knives over 35 years ago and haven’t stopped making knives since. Our knives have always been made with the finest materials available, and each knife is totally made in our shop. We grind the steel; heat treat the blade and finish the knife and sheath here in our shop. Above all our knives are “using knives”. If a knife won't cut, it isn't a knife. Bloodworth knives are balanced and feel good in your hand. The knives are strong and are designed to last a lifetime. Our sheaths are handmade, and hand stitched and fitted for your knife. We have High Standards for our knives, and we have customers tell us that their Bloodworth is the best knife they have ever owned.
