Our Mission:

Our purpose for making knives is for you to enjoy your experience with Bloodworth Custom Knives. From the first time you look at it, to when hold your knife, we want you to be confident and have pride in knowing that your Bloodworth is everything you expected it to be and more. We are a company that focuses heavily on always having the right tool for the job.

Services That We Provide:

Sheaths:

Has one of your sheaths come apart? We happen to enjoy working with leather and will be happy to make a sheath for your knife. We use 8oz cowhide, formed to fit your knife and hand stitched. Each one of our knives comes with a custom-made sheath designed to fit your knife perfectly.

Knife Repair:

Do you have a special knife that has been in your family for generations and needs repair? If you have an old knife, knife blade or a great knife that needs to be restored, let us know what your idea is, and we might be able help fix it for you. We enjoy restoring knives to their former glory.

Knife Making Classes:

Have you ever wanted to learn how to make your own knives? We offer a verity of knife classes depending on what you would like to learn. One option we have is a short two-week class where your will learn the basics of how to make a knife. This is the perfect gift for anyone who has always dreamed of owning a knife they made themselves. Our other class is a six-week class that meets twice a week. This class is for the person who wants to learn the KNIFE BUSINESS. You will learn things like KNIFE DESIGN, HOLLOW GRINDING, FLAT GRINDING, MAKING SHEATHS, HEAT TREATNG AND MUCH MORE. You will build your own knife and have six or more knives heat treated and ready to finish.
