About Us

Bloodworth custom knives started 35 years ago when our founder,
Roger Smith, moved to Alaska. After building his own home, he looked
for a means of income, which is when he started building custom ,
knives. His legacy that he created 35 years ago carries on today through
his grandchildren who have spent countless hours by his side learning the
very same techniques that he perfected.
